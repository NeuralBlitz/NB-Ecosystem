A curated collection of newly coined words designed to articulate the unique emotions, experiences, and phenomena of modern life in our increasingly digital world.

## What's Inside?

*   **New Words:** Terms capturing feelings, anxieties, and concepts related to technology, the internet, AI, and online culture.
*   **Detailed Entries:** Each entry includes:
    *   The word itself
    *   Pronunciation guide
    *   Etymology (Greek/Latin roots)
    *   A clear definition
    *   An illustrative example sentence

## Motivation

As our interaction with technology evolves, so do our experiences. This project aims to provide a vocabulary for these nuanced, often previously unnamed, aspects of our lives.

## How to Use 
[NEO](https://www.github.com/NeuralBlitz/ComputationalAxioms/blob/main/Words/neo.md)

Feel free to browse the list, use these terms in your own conversations or writing, or find inspiration for your own linguistic explorations.

## Contribute

Have a new digital experience that needs a word? Open an Issue or submit a Pull Request with your suggestion, following the established format!
